about me

Trainee junior developer with a background in music, based in Manchester. I took the uncertainty of 2020 as an opportunity to set myself a new challenge.
After making lo-fi pop bangers with my housemates for the first few months of lockdown, and the prescient words of a 1999 David Bowie interview ringing through my head, the path became clear:
I decided to apply myself to the world of coding.
Technology has always fascinated me in its ability to affect change, facilitate community/creativity and solve problems. I am focused on progressing further by starting my first role in development.
